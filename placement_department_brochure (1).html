Department of Fuel, Minerals & Metallurgical Engineering

A Legacy of Energy Leadership

Established at Indian Institute of Technology (ISM) Dhanbad, the Department is one of India's premier centers for technical education and industrial R&D in energy processing, natural resource utilization, and clean technologies.

Fuel & Energy Engineering Vision

The M.Tech program addresses the global dual challenge: ensuring reliable energy security while accelerating net-zero decarbonization. Graduates master both conventional hydrocarbon engineering and next-generation clean tech solutions.

Industry-Integrated Rigor

Combining rigorous thermodynamics, reaction kinetics, and process simulation with hands-on industrial projects, characterization labs, and computational modeling.
